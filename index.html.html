What We Offer
Our Programs

Every student has a place in our program — from first-time musicians to advanced performers ready for the stage.

🎺
Concert Band

Our flagship ensemble performing wind, brass, and percussion literature at concerts and festivals throughout the year.

Grades 6–8 · Intermediate to Advanced
🥁
Beginning Band

The perfect starting point for new musicians. Students learn fundamentals on their chosen instrument in a supportive group setting.

Grade 6 · No Experience Required
🎶
Choir

Vocal training, sight-reading, and choral performance. Students perform at seasonal concerts and community events.

Grades 6–8 · All Voices Welcome
🎻
Orchestra

String ensemble performing classical and contemporary repertoire with opportunities for solo and chamber performance.

Grades 6–8 · Prior String Experience
🎹
Music Theory

Foundational theory, ear training, and composition. Designed to deepen musicianship across all instruments.

Grades 7–8 · Elective Credit
🎤
Jazz Ensemble

Improvisation, swing, and jazz vocabulary. Performs at school events and regional jazz festivals.

Grades 7–8 · Audition Required
